How DraftBridge works

Three steps. No custom integration. No API work.

1

Forward the PO email

Set up auto-forwarding in Gmail, Outlook, or your shared ops inbox. Every wholesale PO email goes to your unique DraftBridge address.

2

DraftBridge matches and validates

DraftBridge reads PDF, CSV, XLSX, or email body. It matches each SKU to your Shopify catalog, checks quantities and pricing, and creates a draft order automatically.

Most POs go straight through. The few that need attention are flagged with exactly what's wrong — unmatched SKU, price discrepancy, ambiguous quantity. Fix once, DraftBridge remembers.
