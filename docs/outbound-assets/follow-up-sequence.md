## When they reply with interest

>

>    or I can send you a direct install link.

## Cadence rules

- Never send more than 3 emails without a reply.
- If they reply negatively, remove from sequence immediately.
- If they reply positively, switch to the sample-PO setup flow.
- Space touches at least 3 business days apart.
- Personalize every touch — never send identical copy to multiple merchants.
