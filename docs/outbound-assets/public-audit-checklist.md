# Merchant Qualification Checklist

Use this to qualify outbound targets before sending cold email.

## Must-have signals (all required)

1. The store is on Shopify (check footer, page source, or BuiltWith).
2. The store has a wholesale or B2B channel — look for:
   - A "Wholesale" or "Trade" page in their navigation
   - A B2B login or account-request form
   - Bulk/case pricing on product pages
   - A "Wholesale inquiries" email address in their footer or contact page
3. The store is roughly $1M–$15M revenue range. Indicators:
   - Multiple product categories with real inventory depth
   - Active social media with brand presence (not a dropshipper)
   - Physical warehouse or fulfillment references
   - Team photos or "about" page showing 5–30 employees

## Strong-fit signals (look for 2+)

4. The store does NOT have heavy ERP/EDI integration visible (no SAP, NetSuite,
   or SPS Commerce references on their careers/tech page).
5. The store shows signs of manual operations:
   - Small team
   - Contact email is a person, not a system
   - No obvious automation stack listed
6. The store is in a wholesale-heavy vertical:
   - Food and beverage
   - Beauty and personal care
   - Apparel (especially private label or DTC+wholesale)
   - Home goods
   - Health and wellness supplements
   - Pet products

## Disqualification signals

- Enterprise with full ERP stack (they already solved this differently)
- Pure DTC with no wholesale channel
- Dropshipping store with no real inventory
- Store appears inactive or low-traffic
- Non-English primary language (v1 is English-only)

## After qualification

- Record the merchant name, domain, wholesale page URL, and contact email
- Draft a personalized first line referencing their wholesale channel
- Send using the founder-email.md template
- Add to the follow-up sequence if no reply within 3 business days
