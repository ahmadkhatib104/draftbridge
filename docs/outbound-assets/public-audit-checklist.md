# Public Audit Checklist

Use this when qualifying outbound targets from the outside.

1. Confirm the store has live reviews and estimate whether review volume is meaningful.
2. Confirm whether Judge.me appears to be the review provider.
3. Check whether a public review policy is easy to find from navigation, footer, or product pages.
4. Check whether imported, incentivized, or syndicated reviews have explicit disclosure.
5. Check whether provenance is visible through labels such as verified buyer or imported.
6. Check whether the storefront shows any trust-language around how reviews are handled.
7. Record whether the store sells into a trust-sensitive or UK-facing context.
8. Decide whether the first angle is:
   - public review policy
   - imported review disclosure
   - provenance visibility
   - monthly controls record
   - agency standardization
