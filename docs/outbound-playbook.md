# Outbound Playbook

## Best First Targets

- Shopify merchants already doing wholesale
- Teams still receiving POs by email or spreadsheet
- Operators re-keying orders manually
- Stores without heavy ERP or EDI workflows

## Demo Motion

1. Ask for one sample PO.
2. Show the forwarded email landing in DraftBridge.
3. Show the resulting draft order in Shopify.
4. Show the internal ops queue for low-confidence cases.
5. Quote time saved and error reduction instead of generic AI language.

## Positioning

- “Email/PDF purchase orders to validated Shopify draft orders.”
- “Forward wholesale POs. Get draft orders. Review only exceptions.”
