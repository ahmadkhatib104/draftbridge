# App Store Listing — DraftBridge

## App Name

DraftBridge — Wholesale PO Intake

## Tagline (80 chars max)

Forward wholesale POs by email. Get validated draft orders in Shopify.

## Short Description (250 chars)

DraftBridge turns emailed wholesale purchase orders into Shopify draft orders
automatically. Forward a PO email with a PDF, CSV, or spreadsheet attached,
and DraftBridge matches SKUs, validates pricing and quantities, and creates
the draft order — or holds uncertain cases for your review.

## Detailed Description

### Stop retyping wholesale purchase orders

If your team still manually enters inbound POs from email, PDF, or
spreadsheet into Shopify, DraftBridge replaces that entire step.

### How it works

**1. Forward the PO email.**
Set up a simple auto-forward rule in Gmail, Outlook, or your shared ops
inbox. Every wholesale PO email — with PDF, CSV, XLSX, or text body — goes
to your unique DraftBridge address.

**2. DraftBridge reads, matches, and validates.**
DraftBridge extracts every line item, matches each SKU to your Shopify
catalog, and checks quantities and pricing against your store data.
High-confidence orders become draft orders automatically. Uncertain orders
stay in a review queue — nothing goes through wrong.

**3. Review only the exceptions.**
Most routine POs go straight through. The small percentage that need
attention are clearly flagged with the specific issue: unmatched SKU,
price discrepancy, ambiguous quantity. Fix it once, and DraftBridge
remembers for next time.

### Why merchants choose DraftBridge

- **Saves 15–30 minutes per wholesale order** — no more retyping from PDF
  to Shopify
- **Reduces data-entry errors** — SKU mismatches, wrong quantities, and
  pricing mistakes are caught before the draft order is created
- **Learns your business** — DraftBridge remembers your customers' SKU
  naming, spreadsheet formats, and product aliases, so accuracy improves
  with every order
- **Works with Shopify B2B** — B2B companies, locations, and wholesale
  price lists are preserved in draft orders, not collapsed into retail
  pricing
- **Transparent confidence scoring** — every order shows exactly why it
  was auto-created or held for review
- **Full audit trail** — every extraction, match, validation, and draft
  order creation is logged

### Supported formats

- Email body text (plain-text POs typed inline)
- PDF attachments (digital and scanned)
- CSV spreadsheets
- XLSX spreadsheets
- Multi-attachment emails (DraftBridge picks the best source automatically)
